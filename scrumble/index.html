CASE FILE: WHY I BUILT SCRUMBLE

"Companies highly value Scrum knowledge, but respected certifications like the PSM I require real understanding, not just rote memorization. I wanted to create a better way to study than flipping through endless flashcards or passively reading guides.

The Scrum Master role is all about removing obstacles, supporting teams, and helping organizations thrive through Sprints. I realized these constraints perfectly mimic a detective's case. So, I built SCRUMBLE: a game that turns the Scrum framework into a playable logic system. Instead of memorizing definitions, you learn to reason through Scrum by solving puzzles. Stop memorizing, and start deducing."
